Mental Health: The Overlooked Epidemic

There have been numerous studies which indicate that social media has a negative impact on the mental health of individuals. Some of the effects include overstimulation, which is decreasing our attention span, stressing us out, and increased feelings of loneliness, isolation, and depression.

What started off as harmless apps for connecting with your friends has now turned into a nightmare. affecting other parts of their lives. In this episode, Aatish and Vikram explore the impact social media has on the individuals that use it.
